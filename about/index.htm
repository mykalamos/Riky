About

Based in London, UK, I play a bansuri derivative made of carbon fibre. This has a greater tolerance of atmospheric extremes and allows for greater fine control whilst retaining a bamboo-like timbre.

I endeavour to carry on the work of my late guru Sri Clive Bell to develop an aesthetic based upon the primacy of Raga elaboration.

I am doing this in the Dagar Dhrupad style - a minimalist, deeply psychological and cerebral gharana that dates back many centuries.

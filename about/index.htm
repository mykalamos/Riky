About

Based in London, UK, I play a bansuri derivative made of carbon fibre. This has a greater tolerance of atmospheric extremes and allows for superior tonal control whilst retaining a bamboo-like timbre.

I am building upon the work of my late guru Pandit Clive Bell to develop a flute aesthetic based upon the sovreignty of Raga with other expressive concerns subservient to that goal.

I am doing this in the Dagar Dhrupad style - a minimalist, psychological and cerebral gharana that dates back many centuries.

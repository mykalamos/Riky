Sri Clive Bell

1939 - 2023

A legend of the bansuri in the UK, Guruji was born in Imperial Japan spending his formative years in the Asia Pacific region before coming to Europe in the 1960s

After visits to India in the 1970s to learn from amongst others Pt Ragunath Seth he left a career in television to pursue Indian Classical music full time

There then followed 40+ years of a life of teaching in London and a versatile performing and recording career

Clive-ji developed a uniquely delicate ornamented playing style with sensitive Raga interpretation its prime objective

He employed a scientific rigour to his teaching writing voluminous notes for his students incuding a treatise on Tihais but all in the service of the spontaneity of the art

As a man he was endowed with a gentle modest demeanour, a mischeivous wit, generous with his time for his students, and possessed of an extensive knowledge of art, literature and other music. He is greatly missed

Oisín Byrne
Kate, 2025
Oil on Board, 77 x 77 cm

From within a square a disciplined chromatic treatise on quietude gently alights upon the viewer. The sitter eyes are closed perhaps on the cusp of an exhalation, inhalation or neither. The shoulders seem tense as the head leans slightly forward. A multitude of candidate emotions are nominated but none gains the majority vote. The off-centre positioning also hints at discomfiture.

You know these things as thoughts, but
your thoughts are not your experiences,

they are an echo and after-effect
of your experiences:

as when your room trembles
when a carriage goes past.

Friedrich Nietzsche
Thus Spoke Zarathustra

Part of a solo exhibition 'Not Marble' at Amanda Wilkinson in London from 10 April to 31 May 2025.

Evie Gazet
Minotaur
Pencil and pen drawing

Meticulous craft is employed to deliver an unsettling conjoined portrait. We are not afforded the usual consolations of surrealism and are instead thrust into an unflinching monochrome vision of a consciousness divided.

The lower head is of an older man inclining his neck downwards. There is a faint touch of malevolence in his pupilless gaze. The beard is lushly drawn with dark shadow in the lower center presaging the horns of the right side of the piece. An ornate broach appears in the lower right placing the personality in Meditteranean antiquity.

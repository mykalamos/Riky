Elspeth Vince
Summons, 2026
Oil on Linen
40cm × 25cm, 45 x 65cm, 40cm × 25cm

As our instincts were sublimated, some havoc became trapped within. It was opined by a Prussian that this results in Gewissenbisse and in its jaws we can start to feel something of this startling triptych.

Squat sentries guard the central panel conceived from a low angle. A naked figure shrouded only by herself locks a single eye on the viewer - the rest of the face obscured by a thicket of limbs. The left hand guards the forehead mimicking compound eyes to ward off predators. The bloodied right hangs to the floor the index finger pointing accusatorily at it. And it is in that hand with wounds still fresh that we witness the bite of conscience.
